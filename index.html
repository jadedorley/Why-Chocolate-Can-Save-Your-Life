Reasons to Eat Chocolate

Chocolate has been scientifically proven to improve your mental and physical health. It is not only tasty but healthy as well. Here are a few reasons why:

  1. Chocolate is good for your blood and heart circulation
  2. Chocolate restores flexibility in your arteries and prevents your arteries from getting clogged which would contribute to heart attacks
  3. Dark chocolate is packed with vitamins such as potassium,zinc and selenium. Also a 100 gram bar of dark chocolate provides 67% iron
  4. Chocolate is good for mothers and infants. Mothers who eat chocolate are less stressed and babies whose mothers eat chocolate smile more often
  5. Dark chocolate has been found to prevent brain injuries and it helps to eat it when studying because chocolate can help with memory retention
  6. PEA or phenethylamine is a chemical found in chocolate in small quantities that stimulates the nervous system and triggers the release of pleasurable opium-like compounds known as endorphins.
